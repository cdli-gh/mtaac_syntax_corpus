For Nanna,
the first-born son
of Enlil,
his beloved master,
Šū-Sîn,
the beloved of Nanna,
the king whom Enlil
in his heart
did choose
as the shepherd of the country
and of the four world quarters,
the strong king,
king of Ur
and king of the four world quarters,
the Murianabak Temple,
his beloved temple,
he built for him.
