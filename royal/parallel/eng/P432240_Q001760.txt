: To Ninmar,
: the queen of the Munus-gilsa temple,
: his mistress,
: for the life
: of Šulgi,
: the mighty man,
: king of Ur
: and king of the four world quarters,
: Ur-Ningirsu,
: (whose second name is) En-mezi-ana,
: the šennu-priest (of Enki)
: (and also) the beloved high priest of Nanše,
: dedicated (this) to her.
