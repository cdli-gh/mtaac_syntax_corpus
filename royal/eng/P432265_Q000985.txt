: Amar-Suena,
: whom in Nippur
: Enlil
: chose by name,
: the constant supporter
: of the temple of Enlil,
: the mighty king,
: king of Ur
: and king of the four world quarters -
: of this statue:
: Amar-Suena is the Beloved of Ur
: is its name.
: Of this statue,
: a person who its standing-place
: shall change,
: and its socle
: shall rip out,
: may Nanna
: the king of Ur,
: and Ningal
: the mother of Ur
: curse him,
: and his seed
: may they bring to an end.
